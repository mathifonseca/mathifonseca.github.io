## Representing the Class of 2013

I had the honor of being selected to deliver the graduation speech on behalf of my fellow Information Systems graduates at Universidad ORT Uruguay's ceremony held at the historic Teatro Solís in Montevideo.

### A Defining Moment

This experience planted the seeds for what would become a lasting passion for teaching and mentorship. Standing before my peers, professors, and families, I reflected on our journey through the program and the opportunities ahead.

### The Beginning of a Teaching Journey

Shortly after graduation, I returned to ORT Uruguay—this time as a professor. Teaching Software Architecture and Cloud Engineering allowed me to give back to the institution that shaped my career and share practical industry experience with the next generation of engineers.

This commitment to education continues today through workshops, mentorship, and knowledge-sharing within every team I lead.
