## Key learnings from our experience

After managing over 100 PSP integrations throughout my career, I compiled the most important lessons into this comprehensive guide published on the Payrails blog.

### Core Topics Covered

**Workflow Flexibility** - Why adopting adaptive methodologies like Kanban helps handle the inevitable roadblocks that come with PSP integrations.

**Team Structure** - The benefits of pairing developers to balance knowledge sharing while maintaining team velocity during complex integrations.

**PSP Timeline Management** - Recognizing that PSPs operate independently and may require unexpected maintenance windows or experience delays outside your control.

**Critical Integration Elements** - Why chargebacks, webhooks, and automated post-authorization operations should be prioritized early in your planning, not treated as afterthoughts.

**Business-Aligned Decisions** - How to select PSPs based on comprehensive business needs, not just API appeal or developer experience.

**User Experience Focus** - Designing intuitive payment interfaces that minimize friction while maintaining security and compliance.

**Communication Strategies** - Using concrete data when addressing PSP issues rather than vague complaints to get faster resolution.

The key takeaway: PSP integration involves far more than connecting API endpoints. It requires strategic planning, organizational discipline, and understanding both technical and business perspectives to succeed.
