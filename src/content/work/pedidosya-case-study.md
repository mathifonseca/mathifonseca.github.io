## Scaling Online Payments at PedidosYa

In this case study video for Spreedly, I explain how PedidosYa tackled the challenge of scaling online payments across Latin America's diverse and fragmented payment landscape.

### The Challenge

PedidosYa operates across multiple countries, each with its own:
- Payment providers and processors
- Preferred payment methods (cards, local wallets, bank transfers)
- Regulatory requirements
- Consumer payment behaviors

Building and maintaining individual integrations for each market wasn't sustainable as we scaled.

### The Solution

By partnering with Spreedly, we were able to:
- **Consolidate integrations** through a single API layer
- **Add new payment methods quickly** as we entered new markets
- **Maintain flexibility** to route transactions to optimal providers
- **Reduce engineering overhead** and focus on core product development

### The Impact

This approach allowed PedidosYa to scale from a regional player to the leading delivery platform across Latin America, processing millions of transactions while maintaining high acceptance rates and a smooth customer experience.
